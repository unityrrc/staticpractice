Prefab ADU vs. Stick Built

Comparing ADU construction options in San Diego

For ADU construction, stick-built shines for those wanting customization, dealing with tricky lots or HOAs, or building multiple larger units. SnapADU specializes in design build with on-site traditional wood framed construction. We offer unmatched value for larger ADUs or multi-unit projects. Meanwhile, a prefab ADU could be a better option for a smaller build or on a spacious, flat lot. Choosing between prefab and a stick-built ADU contractor hinges on your site’s specifics, investment goals, and whether your project calls for a bespoke design. Read more in our guide to prefab ADUs.
